Elephant Ears

Description

Elephant ears are crispy, cinnamon-sugar fried bread confections!

Ingredients

Dough

  • 1 ½ cups of milk
  • ⅜ cup shortening
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of active dry yeast
  • 4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 quart of oil for frying

Topping

  • 6 tablespoons of white sugar.
  • 3 tablespoons of ground cinnamon.

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Prepare dough: Combine milk, shortening, sugar, and salt in a small saucepan over medium heat. Cook and stir until shortening melts and sugar dissolves. Remove from heat and let cool to lukewarm, 110°F (43°C).
  3. Sprinkle yeast into the cooled dough mixture and let sit until foamy.
  4. Transfer mixture to a large bowl and stir in flour to form a dough. Knead until smooth; cover and let rise 30 minutes.
  5. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large saucepan to 375°F (190°C). Roll out 2-inch balls of dough into thin sheets; fry in hot oil until puffed and golden, about 1 to 2 minutes per side.
  6. Drain on paper towels.
  7. Prepare topping: Combine sugar and cinnamon in a bowl.
  8. Sprinkle topping over warm elephant ears and serve.
